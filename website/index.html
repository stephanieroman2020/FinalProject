About our project

We took a csv of all Donald Trump's tweets from when he was inaugurated, January 20th, 2017 up until April 13th, 2017 (the day we ran the data). We ran NLP, sentament analysis, on the tweets. We then combined the data with SPX minute by minute data and found the volatility at certain points after the tweet was released.

We charted a few things along the way as well.

From his official declaration of candidacy in June 2015 through the first two-and-a-half years of his presidency, he tweeted over 17,000 times. Our data set has 16,715 tweets, but only 2,303 were good to use for market hours.
